Frequently Asked Questions


What is your return policy?

Our return policy allows you to return items within 30 days of purchase. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, simply contact our customer support, and we'll guide you through the return process.


How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary depending on your location. Typically, we strive to deliver orders within 5-7 business days. However, please note that delivery times may be affected by external factors such as holidays or unforeseen circumstances.


What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. During the checkout process, you'll be able to choose the payment option that suits you best.


How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a confirmation email containing a tracking number. You can use this tracking number to track your order's progress and estimated delivery date on our website or the respective shipping carrier's website.
